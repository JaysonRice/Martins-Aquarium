Martin's Travel Tips

When rivers heat up in the late summer, some fish slow down and become easier to catch. Make sure you know how the temperature of the water will affect the fish.
Cold water breeds a wild variety of amazing marine life. Durable drysuits are a must for these types of conditions.
Bring a container with the correct conditions for transporting your captured fish. Salt and pH levels matter for making sure your fish stays healthy.
