Martin's Tips

Make sure tank has water with the proper PH levels for your fish.
Clean the tank out on a regular basis.
Make sure to feed them daily, taking into account their diet standards.
pH is an indicator of the acidity or alkalinity of your tank water. Buy a pH test kit to check the pH level.
Plan to replace 25 percent of the water in your aquarium once a month.
